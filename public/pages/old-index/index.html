Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your bone broth unique?

Our bone broth stands out for its exceptional nutritional density and robust flavor profile. Unlike many other brands, we don't water down our broth, ensuring that each sip is packed with essential nutrients and rich, savory taste. Additionally, we are proud to offer the only beverage-focused broth made to drink as-is in New Zealand, providing a convenient and nourishing option for consumers on the go.

What sets your bone broth apart from other products on the market?

Our bone broth stands out for its commitment to quality and authenticity. We source the finest ingredients, including locally sourced bones and organic vegetables, to create a product that is both nutritious and delicious. Our small team is dedicated to handcrafting each batch with care, ensuring that every bottle of bone broth delivers on taste and health benefits.

Is your bone broth suitable for special dietary needs?

Yes, our bone broth is dairy-free, gluten-free, and free from artificial additives or preservatives. It's a versatile option for individuals following various dietary lifestyles, including paleo, keto, and Whole30. Additionally, our commitment to using high-quality, natural ingredients ensures that our bone broth is both nutritious and suitable for a wide range of dietary preferences.

What are the health benefits of consuming your bone broth?

Our bone broth offers a wide range of health benefits thanks to its rich nutritional profile. It is packed with collagen, protein, and essential amino acids that support joint health, promote healthy skin, hair, and nails, aid in muscle recovery and repair, and contribute to overall wellbeing. Additionally, our broth contains minerals like calcium and phosphorus, which are essential for strong bones and teeth, as well as vitamins and minerals that support immune function, energy metabolism, and overall vitality. Regular consumption of our bone broth can help support a healthy lifestyle and contribute to your overall wellness goals.
