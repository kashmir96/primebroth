About our fundraisers

At PrimalPantry our mission is to support the wellness of all kiwis and their pets.

As part of this, we run fundraisers for charities and organisations that are focused on health, wellness and community for both people and pets.

Who can request a fundraiser?

Fundraisers are for any non-profit charity, club or organisation that supports health, wellbeing or community for kiwis and/or their pets.

If you are a for-profit business or influencer, we have other partnership options available, to view these visit our partnership options page

What’s the process?

Once approved, we will provide you with a unique link to include in your social media content, emails, or other communications to promote to your audience. The proceeds collected through this link will be donated to your fundraiser and back to your organisation. It's important to use your unique link and clearly communicate this to your audience, as this is how we track and attribute the proceeds for payout.

What products are included?

By default we limit the fundraiser to bone broth powder range as this is quicker to arrive to your donators and easier all-around logistically. Liquid also has higher shipping costs which will eat into your donations. However, if you would prefer the liquid we can set this up.

How do I share my fundraiser?

Sharing Your Link

There are many ways to share your link and raise funds:

  • Social media posts
  • Social media stories
  • Website banners or links
  • Mass emails or newsletters
  • Selling our products on your website or store using the unique link
