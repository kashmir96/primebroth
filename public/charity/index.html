Primebroth Fundraisers

At Primebroth, one of our core values is to give back to local charities, volunteer groups, and community initiatives supporting the health and wellbeing of both pets and people.

We do this through the Primebroth Fundraising Program, where we donate 100% of the proceeds from the sales of our products to approved organisations each month.

What’s the Process?

Once approved, we will provide you with a unique link to include in your social media content, emails, or other communications to promote to your audience. All proceeds collected through this link will be donated to your fundraiser and back to your organisation. It's important to use your unique link and clearly communicate this to your audience, as this is how we track and attribute the proceeds for payout.

About the product

A great way to add extra nutrition value to your pets' favourite dog biscuits:

  • 100% human grade
  • Organic, no additives
  • Healthy skin and coat
  • Better digestion
  • Strong joints
  • Shelf-stable
Ingredients:

Organic Beef Bones, Carrots, Celery, Apple cider vinegar

Recommended serving size:
  • Small dogs (<10 kg): ½ tsp daily
  • Medium dogs (10-35 kg): 1 tsp daily
  • Big dogs (35 kg +): 1½ - 2 tsp daily
Directions:

Sprinkle on your pet’s food once a day as powder or mix it with hot water first (1 tsp per 100ml).

Servings (depends on pet size):

12g pack - Up to 7 servings; 50g pack - Up to 33 servings; 200g pack - Up to 132 servings

Storage:

Keep in a dry place. Use within 4 months of opening or 24 months unopened.
