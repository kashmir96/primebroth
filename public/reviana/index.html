The actives, without the rest

Reviana uses the ingredients people ask for — peptides, ceramides, hyaluronic acid — in forms chosen with reactive skin in mind.

Most anti-aging products use ingredient forms that can be tough on reactive skin. Reviana uses gentler forms of the same well-known actives, chosen with reactive skin in mind — not a guarantee for everyone, but a considered starting point.

🔬
Peptides
Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 · Eye Cream

Peptides are short chains of amino acids — the building blocks your skin uses to produce collagen. The peptide in Reviana's Eye Cream targets expression lines and crow's feet specifically, supporting the delicate under-eye area.

Targets the appearance of crow's feet and expression lines
Supports collagen and elastin production
Formulated for the delicate under-eye area
🧱
Ceramides
Ceramide NP · Night Cream

Ceramides are the lipids that hold your skin barrier together. Reactive skin often has a depleted ceramide layer, which is part of why it responds to so many things. Replacing them directly helps support the barrier over time.

Helps support the skin's protective barrier
Assists with moisture retention overnight
Chosen for its gentleness on reactive skin
Best used consistently as part of a night routine
💧
Hyaluronic Acid
Sodium Hyaluronate · All four products

One of the most widely used hydration actives in cosmetics — and widely considered one of the gentler options. It attracts and holds water in the skin, helping to plump the appearance of fine dehydration lines.

Helps hydrate and plump the appearance of fine lines
Lightweight — suits oily and combination skin too
One of the more widely tolerated hydration actives
Used across all four Reviana products
