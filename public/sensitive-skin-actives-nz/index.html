Why Reviana exists

Great actives.
Shame about everything else.

Most active-forward serums pack their hero ingredients alongside synthetic fragrance, alcohol solvents, or emulsifier blends that sensitive skin tends to object to. The active isn't the problem — the rest of the formula is.

Reviana formulates around that: Bakuchiol, Azelaic Acid, Niacinamide, Vitamin C and Peptides, carried in a grass-fed tallow lipid base your skin already recognises — with nothing added that we didn't need.

No synthetic fragrance. No harsh solvents. No emulsifier cocktails. Just the actives, in a base that works.

What's inside

Five actives, chosen for sensitive skin.

Each active was selected for its evidence base and its tolerability profile — the ingredients reactive skin can actually use, without the trade-offs.

🌿
Bakuchiol
Retinol Alternative · Night Cream

Shown in peer-reviewed trials to reduce the appearance of fine lines and improve firmness — without the peeling, redness or photosensitivity that makes retinol difficult for reactive skin to use consistently.

  • Clinically shown to reduce visible fine lines
  • No photosensitivity — no need to change your SPF routine
  • Gentler mechanism than retinol — typically better tolerated
⚗️
Azelaic Acid
Even Tone · Serum

One of the few brightening actives clinically recognised for use on sensitive, rosacea-affected and acne-prone skin. Refines texture and evens tone without the irritation profile of AHAs like glycolic or lactic acid.

  • Clinically recognised for rosacea and sensitive skin
  • Reduces post-acne marks and uneven tone
  • No aggressive exfoliation — works with your barrier
✨
Vitamin C — two forms
Brightening · Day Cream + Eye Cream

Kakadu Plum — the world's highest natural Vitamin C source — in the Day Cream. Oil-soluble Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate in the Eye Cream. Both forms chosen for stability and tolerability over standard L-Ascorbic Acid.

  • Kakadu Plum: world's highest natural Vitamin C
  • Antioxidant protection from daily environmental exposure
  • More stable and better tolerated than standard Vitamin C
🔬

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) — in both the Day Cream and the Serum. It stimulates ceramide production, reduces the appearance of pores and supports a calmer, more even complexion. One of the most widely tolerated actives in skincare.

👁️

Caffeine + Peptides — in the Eye Cream. Caffeine targets fluid retention to reduce puffiness. Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 smooths the appearance of expression lines. Both in a lightweight, fragrance-free base designed for the delicate eye area.
