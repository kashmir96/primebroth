How do I prepare it?

Serving as a liquid

  1. 1. Use 12g of broth and mix with 200ml of water.
  2. 2. Measure required serving size of bone broth for your pet size: Small dog (or cat size) - 30ml, Medium dog - 60ml, Large dog - 120ml.
  3. 3. Add a serving of the hydrated bone broth to your pet's biscuits or dinner meal.
  4. 4. Store in the fridge for up to 4 days.

Serving as a powder

  1. Sprinkle 1/2 (small dog or cat) to 1 teaspoon (large dog) of raw bone broth powder to your pet's biscuits or dinner meal
  2. Store powder in a cool dry place

Storage

Keep in a cool dry place.

2 years unopened, 4 months after opening.
