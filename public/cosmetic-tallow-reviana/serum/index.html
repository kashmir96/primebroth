The Science

Glow, tone and hydration — in one formula

Most serums force a trade-off — an acid for brightening, or a humectant for hydration. This formula combines both in a single, stable base that works across all three complexion concerns: more even-looking tone, plumper-looking texture, and deeper hydration.

The bio-identical lipid base carries these actives deeper than water-based serums can reach, supporting better results with less product. Lightweight enough to layer comfortably under both Day and Night Cream.

🍬 Azelaic Acid

A gentle but effective acid shown to support more even-looking tone and reduce post-inflammatory marks — without the irritation risk of AHAs.

🔬 Niacinamide (B3)

Works across multiple pathways — strengthening the barrier, inhibiting melanin transfer, and supporting a calmer-looking complexion.

🌱 Centella Asiatica

The active triterpenoids of Cica. Published research shows meaningful collagen synthesis support for a smoother, more youthful-looking complexion.

💧 Sodium Hyaluronate

Low molecular weight HA that penetrates beyond the surface, delivering lasting plumping and hydration from within.
