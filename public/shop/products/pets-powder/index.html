Powdered Beef Bone Broth.

Struggle to get your pet to eat their kibble? Sick of paying ridiculous prices for expensive meat just to get them to eat? We were too, that's why we made PrimeBroth for pets.

Simply serve on their regular kibble and transform from bland to gourmet.

We're confident they'll lap it up. If they don't, we'll give your money back.

Ingredients

Beef Bones, Carrots, Celery, Apple cider vinegar

Directions

Best served as a meal topper

Serving as a liquid

  1. Mix a serving of powder with 100 ml of hot water and add to their biscuits.

Serving as a powder

  1. Sprinkle a serving of powder onto your pet's biscuits or dinner meal.
Dog Size Dosage
Small dogs (<10 kg) ½ tsp daily
Medium dogs (10-35 kg) 1 tsp daily
Big dogs (35 kg +) 1½ - 2 tsp daily

Storage

Keep in a cool dry place - 2 years unopened, 4 months after opening.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is it made?

Our bone broth is made in Christchurch in our commercial kitchen. We simmer beef bones and vegetables for 32 hours to extract all the nutrients, such as precious collagen.

How long will bone broth powder last?

Refer to the use-by date on the packet.

~2 years unopened, 4 months after opening.

What makes your bone broth unique?

Our bone broth is uniquely dense, setting it apart from many other brands in New Zealand that tend to dilute their products. The rich, gelatinous texture of our broth is a clear indicator of its high quality gained from extra simmering time and careful choosing of the bones. Furthermore, we maintain a balanced acidity by avoiding the common practice of adding large amounts of apple cider vinegar, which can overshadow the broth’s robust, meaty flavour.

What's better bone broth powder or liquid?

Well, it depends what you prefer, convenience of powder, e.g. shelf-stable, easy to use product? At the same time, jars are great for people with large dogs, as the jar is 400 ml and will last you 3-4 days. Lastly, liquid bone broth is richer due to its freshness.
