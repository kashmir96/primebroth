Tallow Ingredients

Grass-fed purified NZ beef tallow, organic olive oil, organic jojoba oil, essential oil.

Bone broth powder ingredients

Beef bones (92%), onions, carrots, celery, apple cider vinegar, salt, spices.

Tallow Directions

Apply a small amount on the face, neck, hands or feet daily in the morning as a moisturiser or evening as a night cream. Best used on slightly damp skin.

Body and facial moisturizer. Eczema cream, monthly hair conditioner.

Bone Broth Directions

Serving size: A single serving is 2 tsp.

How to prepare it:

  • To drink as is - Add two tsp to a cup of hot water, stir and enjoy.
  • To cook with - Use in place of stock or water in your favorite recipe. Either add 2tsp to 250ml of water or add directly as powder.

Tallow Storage

Keep in temperatures below 30 degrees and out of direct sunlight to maintain the shelf life of this balm.

Broth Storage

Keep in a cool dry place - 2 years unopened, 4 months after opening.
