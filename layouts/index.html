Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your bone broth unique?

Our bone broth is uniquely dense, setting it apart from many other brands in New Zealand that tend to dilute their products. The rich, gelatinous texture of our broth is a clear indicator of its high quality gained from extra simmering time and careful choosing of the bones. Furthermore, we maintain a balanced acidity by avoiding the common practice of adding large amounts of apple cider vinegar, which can overshadow the broth’s robust, meaty flavour.

Where can I purchase your bone broth?

You can buy straight from our website, our stalls at the Lyttleton and Ohoka markets or from several pet and health food stores around Christchurch New Zealand.

How to use bone broth for dogs & cats?

Dogs & cats can enjoy our bone broth in various ways, including:

  • Food topper: Pour a small amount over your pet's regular food.
  • Frozen treats: Freeze our bone broth in ice cube trays and use as a refreshing treat or meal topper.
  • On cold/sick days: Serve warm broth to help your sick dog/cat recover and stay hydrated.
  • Training aid: Use as a high-value reward during training sessions.

What are the benefits of bone broth for pets?

Health support: Bone broth is a natural support for joint health, digestion, coat, and liver health of your pet.
Appetite enhancer: It's also a great meal topper thanks to its rich and meaty flavor - even the most picky dogs/cats will eat their food if you sprinkle bone broth on it!
Hydration: Bone broth is an excellent way to keep pets hydrated, especially for those who may not drink enough water. So, it's great for a hot day!
Skin and coat: The collagen in the bone broth contributes to healthy skin and a shiny coat of your pet by supporting the structure and elasticity of the skin.
Nutrient-rich: If you are concerned your dog/cat lacking nutrients, bone broth can help. Rich on protein, bone broth is excellent meal topper for any occasion.

How much bone broth should I feed to my dog/cat?

We recommend the following serving sizes for our bone broth powder:

  • Small dogs (less than 10 kg) - ½ tsp
  • Medium dogs (10-35 kg) - 1 tsp
  • Big dogs (35 kg +) - 1½ - 2 tsp

For our liquid broth, see these serving sizes:

  • Small dogs (less than 10 kg) - 30 ml
  • Medium dogs (10-35 kg) - 60 ml
  • Big dogs (35 kg +) - 1½ - 120 ml
