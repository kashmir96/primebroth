What is naturopathy?

Naturopathy is a holistic approach to health that focuses on treating the whole person rather than just the symptoms.

It blends traditional knowledge with modern science to support the body’s natural ability to heal. A naturopath looks at diet, lifestyle, stress, environment, and individual constitution to uncover the root causes of imbalance. Treatments include nutrition, herbal medicine, lifestyle guidance, and natural therapies designed to restore balance and promote long-term wellbeing.
