About us

Hi there,

We are Curtis and Anna, founders of PrimeBroth.

Nutrition, health and fitness have always been important for us throughout our lives. However, it doesn’t matter how much time or money you invest in your health; it does whatever it wants.

So, Curtis started experiencing joint problems. While searching for the solution, he discovered collagen-rich bone broth that has a joint healing effect. We both added bone broth to our diet since then, yet we also realized its unique nutritional value - dairy and gluten-free and full of protein.

So, we replaced our less natural protein snacks with a bottle of bone broth. We both felt great drinking it daily, but the taste wasn’t as satisfying as its health benefits.

At that point, we decided to make our own bone broth, rich in nutrients and pleasant to drink. Discover our delicious bone broth yourself, and your body will thank you.
