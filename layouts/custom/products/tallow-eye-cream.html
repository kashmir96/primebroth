Ingredients

  • Grass fed tallow
  • Manuka honey

  • Vitamin e
  • Calendula infused oil

Grass fed tallow

We use high grade grass-fed suet, which we wet render and filter 6 times.

Manuka honey

It is highly beneficial for skin due to its natural antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties, which help soothe irritation

Borage seed oil

Borage seed oil is rich in gamma-linolenic acid (GLA), making it excellent for soothing inflammation, restoring moisture, and supporting the skin’s natural barrier—especially beneficial for dry or eczema-prone skin.

Vitamin E

Vitamin E is a powerful antioxidant that helps protect the skin from free radical damage, supports healing, and deeply nourishes to improve skin texture and hydration.

Calendula-infused oil

Calendula-infused oil is known for its soothing and anti-inflammatory properties, helping to calm irritated skin, promote healing, and reduce redness and dryness.

Vitamin C

Vitamin C helps brighten skin and reduce hyperpigmentation.
