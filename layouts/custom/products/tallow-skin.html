Ingredients

Grass-fed NZ beef tallow, organic olive oil, essential oil.
By default we provide lavendar with olive oil, however, if you reach out via our contact form or social media before your order is processed we can organise a different combination using the options below. You can choose main oil: olive, jojoba, avocado and essential oil: lavender, francensent, vanilla, rose, sweet orange, patchouli.

Directions

Apply a small amount on the face, neck, hands or feet daily in the morning as a moisturiser or evening as a night cream. Best used on slightly damp skin.

Body and facial moisturizer. Eczema cream, monthly hair conditioner.

Storage

Keep in temperatures below 30 degrees and out of direct sunlight to maintain the shelf life of this balm.
