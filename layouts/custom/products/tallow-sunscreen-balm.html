Description

Tallow Sunbalm - naturally derived, Mineral tallow sunbalm with SPF15*. Natural, broad-spectrum, no white cast, absorbed by the skin. This tallow sunbalm comes in a 100 ml tin jar.

Key benefits of Tallow sunbalm
  • Absorbs easily without leaving a white cast
  • Fragrance and preservative-free
  • Water-resistant and handmade in small batches
  • Other benefits of tallow: is proven to help with skin conditions like psoriasis, cracked & dry skin, rashes, burning, itching, wrinkles, sun damage, eczema, and more.

DISCLAIMER: This product has not been evaluated according to AS/NZ 2604:2012 Sunscreen products’ standard and is not intended to diagnose, treat or cure any disease or ailment, including sunburn.

Ingredients

Grass-fed/grass-finished tallow, coconut oil, jojoba oil, olive oil, raspberry seed oil, beeswax, 20% non-nano zinc oxide, cacao powder.

Directions

Warm up a small amount between the fingers before applying on the skin. Rub it in gently for about 30 seconds. Apply at least every 2 hours.

Storage

Store in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight to extend the shelf life of the sunbalm.
