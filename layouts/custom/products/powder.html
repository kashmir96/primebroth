Ingredients

All the raw essentials needed for the best bone broth powder

Beef bones (92%), onions, carrots, celery, apple cider vinegar, salt, spices.

May contain Cashew Nuts, Wheat, Gluten.

Grass-fed beef bones (92%)

We use high grade grass-fed beef bones hand picked to have lots of cartilage for better nutrition. These we blanch, roast and simmer for 32 hours to extract all of the goodness.

Directions

Serving size: A single serving is 2 tsp.

How to prepare it:
  • To drink as is - Add two tsp to a cup of hot water, stir and enjoy.
  • To cook with - Use in place of stock or water in your favorite recipe. Either add 2tsp to 250ml of water or add directly as powder.

Keep in a cool dry place - 2 years unopened, 4 months after opening.
