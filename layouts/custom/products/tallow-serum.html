Why kiwis are choosing tallow serum for their skin

Many serums are water-based with synthetic actives suspended in chemical emulsifiers. They sit on the surface, evaporate quickly, and can leave sensitive skin feeling tight or irritated. A tallow serum is different — tallow's fatty acid profile closely mimics our skin's own oils, so the Vitamin C and Hyaluronic Acid are carried deeper rather than just sitting on top.

That's why kiwis are making the switch to tallow serum — real, absorbable actives in a natural base, not a chemical cocktail. Made in small batches in Christchurch by the tallow skincare company featured on Seven Sharp.
