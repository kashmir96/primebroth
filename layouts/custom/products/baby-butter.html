Why NZ parents are choosing tallow for babies

Baby skin is much thinner and more delicate than adult skin — it absorbs more of what you put on it. Many baby creams rely on petroleum-based ingredients that create a film rather than nourishing the skin. Tallow is different — its fatty acid profile closely mimics the oils our bodies naturally produce, so it absorbs rather than just sitting on the surface.

That's why tallow for babies is quickly becoming the go-to for NZ parents who want something genuinely natural — not just marketed as natural. Made in small batches in Christchurch by the tallow skincare company featured on Seven Sharp.
