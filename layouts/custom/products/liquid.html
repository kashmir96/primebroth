Directions

Serving size: 250 ml. Servings per pack: 2

  1. Scrape off the fat cap (we purposely leave this on for added freshness).
  2. Optional, if you find it more gelatinous than you'd like and would prefer liquid, warm it up in the microwave briefly first (note - this is healthy and normal, due to high collagen content).
  3. Drink on its own or use as a part of the recipe.

Storage

Store in the freezer or fridge (up to 30 days).

PrimalPantry should be refrigerated at temperatures below 5 degrees celsius for up to 5 days. Once opened, it should be refrigerated and consumed within 4 days for optimal freshness. You can refrigerate it for up to 30 days if unopened.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is it made?

Our bone broth is made in Christchurch in our commercial kitchen. We simmer beef bones and vegetables for 32 hours to extract all the nutrients, such as precious collagen.

How long will bone broth last?

Refer to the use-by date on the bottle. Once opened, promptly refrigerate unused product at below 5°C and use within 4 days. Do not heat bottle in microwave.

What makes your bone broth unique?

Our bone broth is uniquely dense, setting it apart from many other brands in New Zealand that tend to dilute their products. The rich, gelatinous texture of our broth is a clear indicator of its high quality gained from extra simmering time and careful choosing of the bones. Furthermore, we maintain a balanced acidity by avoiding the common practice of adding large amounts of apple cider vinegar, which can overshadow the broth’s robust, meaty flavour.

What's better bone broth powder or liquid?

Well, it depends what you prefer, convenience of powder, e.g. shelf-stable, easy to use product? At the same time, jars are great for people with large dogs, as the jar is 400 ml and will last you 3-4 days. Lastly, liquid bone broth is richer due to its freshness.

Can pets have your bone broth for people?

If you'd like to give your pets a taste of bone broth goodness, we recommend either our bone broth powder for dogs and cats or the liquid bone broth for pets. Our bone broth for humans has tasty herbs, spices and salt that is fine for people but might upset your pet's bellies.
