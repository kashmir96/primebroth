Most clay masks are too harsh for reactive or easily irritated skin. They pull out impurities but leave you red, tight, and uncomfortable. Our tallow clay mask is different — kaolin clay gently draws out excess oil and impurities, while grass-fed tallow keeps your skin nourished and calm throughout the process.

The result? Clearer, calmer-looking skin with a more even tone — without the redness and irritation that comes with most masks. If you've avoided clay masks because your skin can't handle them, this one was made for you.

Purposeful ingredients, nothing else

Kaolin Clay — The gentlest of all clays. Draws out excess oil and impurities without over-drying or irritating even the most reactive skin.

Grass-Fed Tallow — Rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K. Nourishes and supports the skin barrier while the clay does its work — so you don't come out of a mask looking redder than when you went in.

Aloe Vera — Naturally calming and soothing. Helps keep the skin comfortable during and after masking.

Glycerin — A natural humectant that draws moisture in, so the mask nourishes instead of stripping.

Vitamin E (Tocopherol) — Protects from environmental stressors and supports skin softness.

No synthetic fragrances, no parabens, no harsh chemicals. As with any new skincare product, we recommend a small patch test first.

How to use

Apply a thin, even layer to clean skin, avoiding the eye area. Leave on for 10–15 minutes — you should feel a gentle tightening but no stinging or discomfort. Rinse with warm water and pat dry.

Follow with your Tallow Glow Serum or Tallow Balm for best results — your skin is primed to absorb moisture after masking.

How often

1–2 times per week. If your skin is particularly reactive, start with once a week and see how your skin responds before increasing.
