Ingredients

Beef Bones, Carrots, Celery, Apple cider vinegar

Directions

Best served as a meal topper

1. Scrape off the fat cap (we purposely leave this on for added freshness).

2. Measure out a serving of bone broth. Serving size: small dogs - 30 ml, medium dogs - 60 ml, large dogs - 120 ml

3. Optional, if you find it more gelatinous than you'd like and would prefer liquid, warm in microwave briefly first (note - this is healthy and normal, due to high collagen content).

4. Optional, mix in with water if you want your pets bone broth to go further.

5. Pour bone broth onto your pets' biscuits or dinner meal.

Small dogs & cats ( under 10 kg) - 30 ml

Medium dogs (10-35 kg) - 60 ml

Big dogs (35 kg +) - 120 ml

Storage

Store in the freezer.

Safe to freeze. PrimeBroth should be refrigerated at temperatures below 5 degrees celsius for up to 5 days. Once opened, it should be refrigerated and consumed within 4 days for optimal freshness.
