How to use

Apply a small amount on the face, neck, hands or feet daily in the morning as a moisturiser or evening as a night cream. Best used on slightly damp skin.

Also great as a body moisturiser, eczema cream, or monthly hair conditioner.

For best texture and consistency, keep between 18–25°C and out of direct sunlight.
