The Science

Why overnight repair matters

Your skin's cell regeneration peaks while you sleep. Most night creams pile on heavy synthetics that clog pores and irritate sensitive skin. Reviana Night Cream uses bio-identical lipids that work with your skin's natural repair cycle — absorbing deeply to deliver restorative actives exactly when your skin needs them most.

Wake up to visibly firmer, smoother, more radiant skin — without the irritation.

🌿 Bakuchiol

A natural retinol alternative shown in peer-reviewed trials to visibly reduce the look of fine lines and improve firmness — without peeling or photosensitivity.

🧱 Ceramide NP

Ceramides make up roughly 50% of the skin's lipid matrix. Restores barrier integrity and significantly reduces water loss overnight.

💧 Sodium Hyaluronate

Low molecular weight HA penetrates deeper than standard hyaluronic acid, delivering lasting hydration beneath the surface.

🔬 Niacinamide (B3)

Clinically studied to reduce the look of fine lines, improve elasticity, and even skin tone. Works with your overnight repair cycle.
