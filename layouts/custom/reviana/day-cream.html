The Science

Why bio-identical lipids change everything

Most moisturisers sit on top of your skin. Reviana is built around lipids that mirror your skin's own structure — absorbing deeply and delivering actives where they actually make a difference for tone, texture and radiance.

Brighter, more hydrated, more even-looking skin — with a finish that works under makeup.

🧬 Bio-Identical Lipids

Tallow and Squalane mirror your skin's own lipid structure — absorbing deeply and carrying actives where surface-level moisturisers can't reach.

✨ Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate

Oil-soluble Vitamin C that penetrates the skin barrier more effectively than standard Vitamin C, for brighter-looking tone without stinging.

🌿 Niacinamide (B3)

One of the most studied actives in skincare. Supports a calmer complexion and inhibits melanin transfer for more even-looking tone.

🍑 Kakadu Plum

The world's highest known natural source of Vitamin C. Antioxidant protection alongside brightening and complexion-evening benefits.
