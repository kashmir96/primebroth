Each active was chosen for what it can do for tone, texture, radiance and the visible signs of aging — in forms that work with the ultra-purified tallow base, not against it.

🌿
Bakuchiol
Overnight renewal · Night Cream

Bakuchiol supports smoother-looking skin, refined texture and softer-looking fine lines over time. It delivers many of the beauty results associated with retinol — in a gentler format that suits a lipid-rich base like ultra-purified tallow.

  • Supports smoother-looking skin overnight
  • Helps soften the look of fine lines with consistent use
  • Gentler than retinol — suitable for sensitive skin
✨
THD Ascorbate
Brightening Vitamin C · Day Cream, Eye Cream, Night Cream

THD Ascorbate is a stable, oil-soluble form of Vitamin C chosen for brightness, glow and more even-looking tone. It pairs naturally with a lipid-rich base — making it an ideal fit for every Reviana formula it appears in.

  • Supports brighter, more radiant-looking skin
  • Helps improve the look of uneven tone over time
  • More stable and better tolerated than standard Vitamin C
🧱
Ceramide NP
Barrier nourishment · Night Cream

Ceramide NP supports skin comfort, moisture retention and a smoother-looking surface. In Reviana, it works alongside the ultra-purified tallow base to help create the nourished, resilient feel that gives skin a fresher, healthier-looking finish by morning.

  • Helps skin feel nourished and comfortable
  • Supports lasting hydration overnight
  • Promotes a smoother-looking skin surface
🔬

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) is included across all four products. It supports more even-looking tone, a calmer-looking complexion and a smoother overall skin finish — one of the most versatile and well-tolerated actives in skincare.

💧

Hyaluronic Acid is in all four products. It draws in and holds moisture beneath the surface, supporting plumper-looking, fresher-feeling skin with a more hydrated finish throughout the day and overnight.
