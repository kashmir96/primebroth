Bakuchiol
Overnight renewal · Night Cream

Supports smoother-looking skin and softer-looking fine lines over time. Delivers many of the beauty results associated with retinol — in a gentler format suited to a lipid-rich base like ultra-purified tallow.

THD Ascorbate
Brightening Vitamin C · Day Cream, Eye Cream, Serum

A stable, oil-soluble form of Vitamin C chosen for brightness, glow and more even-looking tone. Pairs naturally with a lipid-rich base for better penetration and less irritation than standard Vitamin C.

Supports skin comfort, moisture retention and a smoother-looking surface. Works alongside ultra-purified tallow to create the nourished, resilient feel that leaves skin looking fresher by morning.
