The Science

Targeted brightening for the eye area

Dark circles, fine lines and puffiness around the eye are driven by thinning skin, poor circulation and collagen loss. Stabilised Vitamin C works on all three — supporting collagen production, brightening the look of hyperpigmentation, and protecting against environmental stress.

Paired with Caffeine for a fresher-looking appearance and bio-identical lipids for deeper delivery — targeted brightening and renewal for the area that shows it first.

✨ THD Ascorbate

Oil-soluble Vitamin C that penetrates the delicate eye area without stinging. Supports brighter-looking under-eyes and helps even dark circles.

☕ Caffeine

A well-studied vasoconstrictor that improves microcirculation. Measurable support for a less puffy, more awake-looking appearance.

🧬 Acetyl Hexapeptide-8

A signal peptide that relaxes facial muscle contractions. Visible improvement in crow's feet depth within 30 days.

💧 Hyaluronic Acid

Low molecular weight HA that penetrates deeper than standard hyaluronic acid, for plumper-looking skin under the eye.
