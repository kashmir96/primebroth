Delivery

At PrimeBroth, we strive to provide efficient and reliable delivery services to ensure that you receive your bone broth products in a timely manner. Please read the following information regarding our delivery options and policies:

Delivery Options

We offer the following delivery options for orders placed on our website:

  • Standard Delivery: Orders are typically delivered within 3-5 business days.
  • Express Delivery: For customers who require faster delivery, we offer express shipping options with delivery within 1-2 business days.

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates vary depending on the delivery option selected, as well as the destination of the order. Shipping costs will be calculated and displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Order Processing

All orders are processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days of payment confirmation. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

Delivery Restrictions

Please note that we currently only offer delivery within New Zealand. We are unable to deliver to PO boxes or international addresses at this time.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been dispatched, you can track its progress using the tracking information provided in your confirmation email. If you have any questions or concerns about the status of your delivery, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding delivery, please contact our customer support team at hello@primebroth.co.nz. We're here to help!
