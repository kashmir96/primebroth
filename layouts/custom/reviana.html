Ingredients you'll actually recognise

Reviana uses the actives people ask for — peptides, ceramides, hyaluronic acid — in forms that work for reactive skin instead of against it.

Most anti-aging products work by causing a degree of skin stress — that's fine for normal skin, but for sensitive skin it triggers redness and flares. Reviana uses gentler forms of the same well-known actives that get you the results without the reaction.

💉
Peptides
Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 · Eye Cream

Peptides are short chains of amino acids — the building blocks your skin uses to produce collagen. The peptide in Reviana's Eye Cream targets expression lines and crow's feet specifically, signalling skin to firm and smooth the delicate under-eye area without any irritation.

Visibly reduces crow's feet and expression lines
Supports collagen and elastin production
Works on the most delicate skin on your face
No stinging or sensitivity — ever
🧱
Ceramides
Ceramide NP · Night Cream

Ceramides are the lipids that hold your skin barrier together. Sensitive skin typically has a depleted ceramide layer — which is why it reacts to everything. Replacing them directly helps rebuild the barrier from within, reducing reactivity over time rather than just masking it.

Restores the skin's protective barrier
Locks in moisture through the night
Reduces reactivity to environmental triggers
Works best used consistently as a night treatment
💧
Hyaluronic Acid
Sodium Hyaluronate · All four products

Hyaluronic acid is one of the most recognised hydration actives in skincare — and one of the most gentle. It attracts and holds water in the skin, visibly plumping fine lines from dehydration. Reviana uses the sodium salt form, which penetrates deeper for more sustained hydration throughout the day and night.

Plumps and smooths fine dehydration lines
Deeply hydrating without heaviness or grease
Suited to all skin types including oily and combination
Boosts absorption of other actives
