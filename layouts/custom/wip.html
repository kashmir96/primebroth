Directions

People's broth directions

Serving size: A single serving is 2 tsp.

How to prepare it:

  • To drink as is - Add two tsp to a cup of hot water, stir and enjoy.
  • To cook with - Use in place of stock or water in your favorite recipe. Either add 2tsp to 250ml of water or add directly as powder.
Pet's broth directions

Best served as a meal topper

Serving as a liquid

  1. Mix a serving of powder with 100 ml of hot water and add to their biscuits.

Serving as a powder

  1. Sprinkle a serving of powder onto your pet's biscuits or dinner meal.
