About the product

PrimalPantry is a high quality bone broth powder designed for pets and people.

For people: Coming soon.

For pets:

A great way to get picky pets to take an interest and enjoy their kibble while adding extra nutrition value:

  • 100% human grade
  • Organic, no additives
  • Transforms pets from picky to piggy
  • Healthy skin and coat
  • Better digestion
  • Strong joints
  • Shelf-stable
Ingredients:

Organic Beef Bones, Carrots, Celery, Apple cider vinegar

Recommended serving size:
  • Small dogs (<10 kg): ½ tsp daily
  • Medium dogs (10-35 kg): 1 tsp daily
  • Big dogs (35 kg +): 1½ - 2 tsp daily
Directions:

Sprinkle on your pet’s food once a day as powder or mix it with hot water first (1 tsp per 100ml).

Servings (depends on pet size):

12g pack - Up to 7 servings; 20g pack - Up to 12 servings; 50g pack - Up to 33 servings; 200g pack - Up to 132 servings

Storage:

Keep in a dry place. Use within 4 months of opening or 24 months unopened.
