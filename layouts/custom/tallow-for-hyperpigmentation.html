Common Questions

How long until I see results?

Most customers notice improved skin texture and radiance within 2-3 weeks. Visible fading of hyperpigmentation typically takes 6-12 weeks of consistent use, as this requires multiple cycles of cell turnover. Patience and consistency are key.

Is this safe to use during pregnancy?

Yes! Our tallow products contain no retinoids, harsh chemicals, or ingredients of concern during pregnancy. Many women use tallow specifically for pregnancy-related melasma as it's both safe and effective.

Can I use this alongside other treatments?

Tallow works beautifully alongside most skincare. However, if using prescription treatments like hydroquinone or tretinoin, we recommend consulting your dermatologist about how to incorporate tallow into your routine.

Will this work on old hyperpigmentation?

Yes, though older, more established pigmentation may take longer to fade. The key is consistent application and protecting skin from further sun damage. Many customers see improvement even on spots they've had for years.
