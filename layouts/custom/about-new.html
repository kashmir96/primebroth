Meet our founder

G’day, I’m Curtis!

I'm a Christchurch local and software marketer turned boutique broth and balm business owner.

PrimalPantry began as Primebroth, a bone broth company focused on giving kiwis affordable access to protein and collagen through other means than typical highly processed supplements.

We’ve been in business about a year now, attending markets every week and learning from our customers–and we’ve learned a lot! Hundreds of hours talking to home cooks, health and wellness enthusiasts, people into natural remedies and other niche topics have shaped the business into what it is today.

With these learns we’ve continually developed our product and added additional lines based on our customer’s needs. We broadened our mission to focus on helping kiwi’s wellness on the inside and out using our refined take on recipes lost from past generations.

Meet the PrimalPantry line up!

Since the original days of our frozen broth, the product line has expanded to include other natural products derived from animal byproducts also left behind in most mass farming.

We separated these into two sub-brands: Primebroth with beef and chicken bone broth powder and Primalcare for our tallow balms.

All 100% made by hand by us in Christchurch.

Our ingredients are sustainably and responsibly sourced

All of our products are focused on leveraging parts of animals often overlooked and going to waste. If an animal is being processed for eating, it’s our responsibility as a society to ensure as much of the animal is being utilised well.

That being said, we also ensure our products are the best quality on the market, and as such we source only the best quality ingredients like suet and bones from 100% grass fed and nz grown beef, or organic chicken frames.
