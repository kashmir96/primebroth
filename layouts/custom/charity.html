Primebroth for Charities

At Primebroth, one of our core values is to give back to local charities, volunteer groups, and community initiatives supporting the health and wellbeing of both pets and people.

We do this through the Primebroth Charity Program, where we donate 100% of the proceeds from the sales of our products to approved charities each month.

Whatâ€™s the Process?

Once approved, we will provide you with a unique link to include in your social media content, emails, or other communications to promote to your audience. All proceeds collected through this link will be donated to your charity. It's important to use your unique link and clearly communicate this to your audience, as this is how we track and attribute the proceeds for payout.

Sharing Your Link

There are many ways to share your link and raise funds:

  • Social media posts
  • Social media stories
  • Website banners or links
  • Mass emails or newsletters
  • Selling our products on your website or store using the unique link
See how to share with your audience

Ready to Raise Funds Together?

To apply, send an email to hello@primebroth.co.nz with details about your charity. We will get in touch for more information.
