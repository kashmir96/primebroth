What makes your bone broth unique?

Our bone broth is uniquely dense, setting it apart from many other brands in New Zealand that tend to dilute their products. The rich, gelatinous texture of our broth is a clear indicator of its high quality gained from extra simmering time and careful choosing of the bones. Furthermore, we maintain a balanced acidity by avoiding the common practice of adding large amounts of apple cider vinegar, which can overshadow the broth’s robust, meaty flavour.

About the product

A great way to add extra nutrition value to your pets' favourite dog biscuits:

  • 100% human grade
  • Organic, no additives
  • Healthy skin and coat
  • Better digestion
  • Strong joints
  • Shelf-stable
Ingredients:

Organic Beef Bones, Carrots, Celery, Apple cider vinegar

  • Big dogs (35 kg +): 1½ - 2 tsp daily

Sprinkle on your pet’s food once a day as powder or mix it with hot water first (1 tsp per 100ml).

Servings (depends on pet size):

12g pack - Up to 7 servings; 50g pack - Up to 33 servings; 200g pack - Up to 132 servings

Storage:

Keep in a dry place. Use within 4 months of opening or 24 months unopened.

About the product

A great way to add extra nutrition value to your pets' favourite dog biscuits:

  • 100% human grade
  • Organic, no additives
  • Healthy skin and coat
  • Better digestion
  • Strong joints
  • Shelf-stable
Ingredients:

Organic Beef Bones, Carrots, Celery, Apple cider vinegar

Usage instructions:

Serving as a liquid:

  1. Use 12g of broth and mix with 200ml of water.
  2. Measure required serving size of bone broth for your pet size: Small dog (or cat size) - 30ml, Medium dog - 60ml, Large dog - 120ml.
  3. Add a serving of the hydrated bone broth to your pet's biscuits or dinner meal.
  4. Store in the fridge for up to 4 days.

Serving as a powder:

Sprinkle 1/2 (small dog or cat) to 1 teaspoon (large dog) of raw bone broth powder to your pet's biscuits or dinner meal.

Store powder in a cool dry place.

Recommended serving size:
  • Small dogs (<10 kg): ½ tsp daily
  • Medium dogs (10-35 kg): 1 tsp daily
  • Big dogs (35 kg +): 1½ - 2 tsp daily
Servings (depends on pet size):

12g pack - Up to 7 servings; 50g pack - Up to 33 servings; 200g pack - Up to 132 servings

Storage:

Keep in a dry place. Use within 4 months of opening or 24 months unopened.

How to Allow Us to Boost Your Posts on Facebook/Instagram

To allow PrimeBroth to boost your posts on Facebook and Instagram, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Page or Instagram profile.
  2. Find the post you want PrimeBroth to boost.
  3. Click on the post's settings or options (usually represented by three dots or a gear icon).
  4. Select "Allow Boosting" or "Promote Post."
  5. In the settings, look for an option to add collaborators or partners.
  6. Add PrimeBroth's Facebook Page or Instagram account as a collaborator or partner.
  7. Save your settings and notify PrimeBroth that the post is ready for boosting.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact us at support@primebroth.co.nz.
