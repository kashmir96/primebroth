Description

Vitallow Organic Beef Tallow is made from organic fat supplied by The Organic Farm Butchery.

This nutrient-rich tallow is loaded with essential fat-soluble vitamins, such as A, D, E, and K2.

Ideal for high-temperature cooking, tallow’s high smoke point ensures stability.

Though not certified organic due to the abattoir’s status, all animals are sourced from certified organic farms in Hawkes Bay.
